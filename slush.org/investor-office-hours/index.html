Index Ventures – Off the Record with Adyen and Index: Q&A with Pieter van der Does & Jan Hammer

After they come off stage the Founder and CEO of Adyen, Pieter van der Does and Partner at Index Ventures, Jan Hammer will be meeting with a small group of founders for an off the record conversation.

This is an invite only session but we’ve reserved 5 spots for you to join the conversation – apply now to secure your spot.

Speedinvest – The first million is the hardest - from idea to €1m to €10m in ARR

Markus Lang, lead partner on Speedinvest’s SaaS & Infra team sits down with Lisa Smith, Founder & CEO of Prewave, one of the fastest growing B2B SaaS companies in Europe right now to discuss their path from inception to exponential growth.

Join for an interactive session that will combine Lisa’s own unique story of raising over €30m from Europe’s best funds with actionable advice. You will leave this session with lessons learned from finding product market fit, defining a sales strategy, and which values and metrics to focus on to steer your company in the right direction from the very beginning.

This is an invite-only event but we’ve reserved 10 spots for you to join the conversation. Apply now to secure your seat at the table.

Target Global – Focusing on customer engagement and retention in B2C Companies

Come and meet the investors at Target Global as they share their insights on how consumer startups should think about measuring and driving user engagement as well as best practices with respect to customer retention to be attractive to venture investors.

 

Apply to join the session

Inventure X 3VC X Practica Capital – The Baltic Playbook

The Baltics are evolving, fast. Join Inventure, 3VC, and Practica Capital to discuss the new playbook for building category leaders out of the region, featuring special guests. This event includes lunch and is targeted to Baltic founders. Welcome!

Antler – Building from Day Zero

Antler is an early-stage venture capital now launching their first local cohort in Helsinki.

Whether you’re a seasoned serial entrepreneur or an aspiring one dreaming of building your first venture, join our team to discuss the crucial first decisions a founder needs to make when starting to build.

Our founders join Antler to strengthen their business models, some to find a team to build with, and to get access to our strong global network of advisors coaching them on their journey to become the next big thing.

Spintop Ventures – Female Founders Forum

Diversity is a superpower Spintop has been investing in since 2010. We are currently investing from our fourth fund and can proudly say that 40% of our portfolio companies are led by female CEOs.

Whether you’re a female founder seeking investment, mentorship, or valuable connections, this event is for you.

Join us for a session where we share our best advice, insights from the remarkable women we have backed, and invite you to an open Q&A with us.

FAQ

Where will Investor Office Hours take place?

The Investor Office Hour session will take place at the main event inside the dedicated private venue.

Why can't everyone join the sessions?

The session program is tailored for specific audiences, and due to the sessions being held at a private venue, there is a maximum limit on the number of guests who can be invited.

How can I join the sessions?

Among the sessions, there are those that operate on an invitation basis, while others welcome open applications for participation.

You can submit your application to participate in sessions that feature the green “Apply to join the session” button. The audience is carefully selected from among the applicants, and those selected will receive email notifications.

I got invited to a session but can't come.

If you receive an invitation but are unable to attend the session, kindly reach out to [email protected]. This will allow us to offer the spot to someone eager to participate.
