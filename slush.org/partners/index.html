Grand visibility — Partner Booth

“We even got some new deal closed at the booth.”

Partner Booth gives you a 100% customizable, large-scale visibility among the world’s most relevant startup world audience. You can even book meetings directly to your Partner Booth with our Matchmaking tool.

We are known for not selling just plain visibility. Our Partner Booths bring concrete help for founders and drive the best business results. We can create anything from a mini stage showcasing your team’s cutting-edge program, to invite-only lounges, to mentoring sessions.

What do I do?

All your qualified leads at one event

Every other person you come across at Slush is a founder, and every fourth person is a VC investor. The rest are corporate executives, policymakers, and researchers. No other startup event makes every lead a qualified lead.

3 out of 4 partners in 2023 met all their business goals at Slush. Our lead generation tool built into the Slush app and badges ensures that you walk home with business results.

Catch your core audience with Partner Side Events

Attending Side Events is the 2nd most popular activity at Slush, directly after meeting people. Our partners get the chance to organize exclusive Side Events in the Partner Side Event Wing – right next door to the Slush Main Event. Side Events are our partners’ favorite tool to cater to a more targeted audience ranging from 50 to 350 visitors.

Partner Side Events are a turnkey service. We produce the venue, help set up the venue with volunteers, and send direct invites to your ideal audience of Slush visitors to make sure that the event is a success.

Build Slush Magic outside of Messukeskus

Aside from the extravaganza taking place in the halls of Messukeskus, a key part of Slush are all the Side Events taking place around Helsinki during the same week.

Anyone is welcome to host a Community Side Event in Helsinki during Slush for free. Providing this opportunity has been one of the core building blocks of what Slush brings to the ecosystem – and will be in 2023 too.

How do I list a Community Side Event?

It’s simple – find your way to Slush Platform:

  1. Open “My events” from the left-hand bar
  2. Open the green “Create an event” tap in the top right corner
  3. Choose “Create Community Side Event”
  4. Fill in the application and submit
  5. We will review your application and get back within a week
  6. When all is good and clear, the event will listed under “Other Side Events” on Slush Platform
