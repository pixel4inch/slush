    Come to meet the best emerging managers on the continent.

100+ diverse LPs from around the world

Slush 2023 will host 100+ LPs representing university endowments, fund of funds, pension funds, sovereign wealth funds, and family offices from around the world.

We’ll arrange exclusive LP-only Side Events to connect you with other LPs, help you exchange notes on managers, and provide you with a relaxed setting to discuss the ecosystem.

The LP events will be invitation only gatherings for Slush’s LP ticket holders.

Unique insights with our Matchmaking Tool

Our Matchmaking Tool helps you understand who is coming. Export fund data and pitch decks, and reach out to GPs you would like to meet. Stay tuned for the release of our Matchmaking Tool for Slush 2023.

We're here to help

Our LP team will be at your disposal to help you navigate the event, set up meetings with relevant managers, and curate bespoke GP lists according to your investment criteria.

Please don’t hesitate to reach out to us by emailing our LP Relations at [email protected].

Slush 2023 brings RAISE to Europe

Twenty high caliber European emerging managers (max raising fund no III) will be selected to attend after an application process. The GPs will be curated by a selection committee composed of LGT, Axa, Top Tier, Foobar, Isomer Capital, and Blue Future Partners.

WHEN: Slush Day 2 (Dec 1st)
FOR WHOM: Slush LP ticket holders, 20 selected European emerging GPs.
FORMAT: GPs will present very short pitches, followed by networking. Food and drinks will be served.
WHERE: Slush main venue
