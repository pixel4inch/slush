MIKI KUUSI • 

Miki Kuusi is the CEO of Wolt and Head of DoorDash International. Founded in 2014, Wolt is a technology company that makes it incredibly easy to discover and get the best restaurants, grocery stores and other local shops delivered home or to the office. Wolt joined forces with DoorDash in 2022 and operates in 27 countries today, 23 of which are with the Wolt product and brand. Previously, Miki was the CEO of Slush, one of the leading tech and investor events in the world attended by more than 25,000 people annually. Wired listed Miki as #32 on their top technology influencers in Europe for 2015, while Forbes featured Miki on their 30 under 30 list for 2016.
